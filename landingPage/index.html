What is Calisthenics?

Calisthenics is a form of fitness focused on using your body weight for resistance. Improve your strength, flexibility, and endurance with minimal equipment. Calisthenics originated in Ancient Greece and became popular again in the early 19th century. Pushups, squats, crunches, planks, burpees, pullups, and lunges are all examples of calisthenics.
